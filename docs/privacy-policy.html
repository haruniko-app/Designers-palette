Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Designer's Palette for Google Slides™ collects, uses, and protects information when you use our add-on.

1. Information We Access

  • Presentation Content: We access images, shapes, text, and formatting to provide editing features.
  • Selected Elements: Position, size, color, and style of selected objects.
  • Image Data: When using AI features, image data is temporarily transmitted to cloud servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • Display and modify presentation elements as requested
  • Process images using AI features when initiated
  • Provide color, alignment, sizing, and formatting features

3. Data Storage

  • Local Processing: Most operations are performed locally. No data is stored on external servers.
  • AI Processing: Image data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored.
  • Browser Storage: Preferences may be stored locally on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or transfer your information to third parties, except:

  • AI Processing: Image data is processed using Google Cloud services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • AI processing uses secure, isolated environments
  • No persistent storage of user data on our servers

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Your data remains in your Google Slides presentations.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling removes all associated browser storage.
  • Control: You can choose not to use AI features.

7. Children's Privacy

The add-on is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be indicated by the "Last Updated" date.
